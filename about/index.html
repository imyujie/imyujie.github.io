About YUJIE

I'm a product designer based in Beijing, currently working at ByteDance. Prior to ByteDance, I worked at Microsoft as a UX Designer for 1.5 years, during which I spent most of my time designing Bing News-related products.

Education

I got my bachelor degree of Software Engineering in 2017. During my student life, I loved building products on my own. But gradually I found my passion on designing digital products and began my design career after the internship at a tiny startup company.

Experience

I joined Microsoft as UX Designer right after my graduation. I was responsible for Twitter answer as well as mobile news experience on Bing. After that, I joined ByteDance, the company owns the most popular short-form video app - TikTok. I'm the lead designer for our design system.
